About Us

Precision Engineering
Since 2009

Deright Precision Machinery Co., Ltd. (德瑞精密機械有限公司) is a Taiwan-based manufacturer specialising in advanced powder processing equipment. Since 2011, we have been authorized by Ohkawara Kakohki Co., Ltd. (Japan) to manufacture water-based spray dryer systems — expanding in 2013 to cover large-scale production machines.

We also serve as the exclusive distributor for W.A.B. DYNO®-MILL bead mills and TURBULA® 3D mixers from Switzerland, providing a complete powder processing solution from R&D through full-scale production.

Our quality policy centres on teamwork, empathy, and excellence — constantly improving to create high-performance, high-reliability, low-labour, low-maintenance equipment that exceeds customer expectations.
